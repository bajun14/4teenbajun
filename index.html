About

Indo Danish Tool Room (IDTR), Jamshedpur is a leading institution dedicated to technical excellence and industrial skill development.

Established with Danish collaboration, IDTR is known for providing industry-relevant training in tool design, CNC machining, mechatronics, and automation.

The institution emphasizes practical training, innovative teaching, and hands-on experience to prepare students for the real-world demands of the manufacturing and engineering sectors.
